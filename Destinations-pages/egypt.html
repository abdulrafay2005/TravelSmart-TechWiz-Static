Egypt

OVERVIEW

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders, where history and culture thrive. Famous for the iconic pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, Egypt offers a fascinating journey through time. Visitors can explore the Nile River, which has been the lifeblood of the country for millennia, and experience the bustling streets of Cairo, rich with modern and traditional influences.

Whether you're diving in the Red Sea, visiting the temples of Luxor, or exploring the Valley of the Kings, Egypt is a destination that promises adventure and discovery at every turn.

Transportation in Egypt

Air Travel

Major international airports include Cairo International Airport (CAI) and Hurghada International Airport (HRG).

Public Transit

Buses and the Cairo Metro provide extensive public transportation options in urban areas.

Car Rental

Rent a car from local and international companies available in major cities.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Uber and local taxis are popular and widely used in Cairo and other urban centers.

Location of Egypt

Egypt is located in northeastern Africa, bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north, the Red Sea to the east, and the Sahara Desert to the west. It's known for its ancient civilization and monumental landmarks.
