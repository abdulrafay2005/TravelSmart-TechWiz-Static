Transportation in Dubai

Air Travel

Dubai International Airport (DXB) is one of the busiest airports in the world.

Public Transit

The Dubai Metro offers efficient transport around the city, along with buses and trams.

Car Rental

Numerous car rental services are available for exploring Dubai and beyond.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Taxis are widely available, and ride-sharing apps like Uber operate in the city.

Location of Dubai

Dubai is located on the southeast coast of the Arabian Peninsula, within the emirate of the same name. It is part of the United Arab Emirates and is bordered by Abu Dhabi to the south and Sharjah to the northeast.
