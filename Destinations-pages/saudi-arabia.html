Saudi Arabia

OVERVIEW

Saudi Arabia, known for its rich history and cultural heritage, is home to the two holiest cities in Islam, Mecca and Medina. With vast deserts, modern cities, and a blend of tradition and innovation, it offers a unique travel experience. Visitors can explore the ancient Nabatean city of Al-Ula, the stunning Red Sea coastline, and the vibrant capital, Riyadh.

Whether you're interested in historical sites, outdoor adventures, or experiencing local culture, Saudi Arabia is a destination that promises to captivate and inspire.

Transportation in Saudi Arabia

Air Travel

Major international airports include King Khalid International Airport (RUH) in Riyadh and King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) in Jeddah.

Public Transit

Public buses and metro services are available in major cities like Riyadh and Jeddah.

Car Rental

Car rental services are widely available, making it easy to explore the country.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Taxis and ride-sharing apps like Uber and Careem are popular for getting around.
