OVERVIEW

India is a vast and diverse country known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. From the majestic Himalayas in the north to the serene beaches of Goa in the south, India offers a myriad of experiences. The bustling cities of Delhi and Mumbai showcase modernity alongside traditional markets, while the historical sites like the Taj Mahal and Jaipur’s palaces narrate tales of a glorious past.

Nature lovers can explore the lush tea gardens of Darjeeling, the deserts of Rajasthan, or the backwaters of Kerala. Culinary enthusiasts will delight in the varied and flavorful cuisine, reflecting the country's diverse cultures. Whether you’re on a spiritual journey in Varanasi or seeking adventure in Rishikesh, India promises an unforgettable experience.

Transportation in India

Air Travel

Major international airports include Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL) in Delhi and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM) in Mumbai.

Public Transit

Metro systems, buses, and auto-rickshaws are widely available in major cities.

Car Rental

Car rentals are available through various companies in major cities, but driving may be challenging for newcomers.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Uber and Ola are popular ride-sharing options in urban areas.

Accommodation Options

Location of India
