OVERVIEW

Pakistan is a land of diverse landscapes, rich culture, and historical significance. From the towering peaks of the Karakoram Range, including K2, to the lush green valleys of Swat and Hunza, Pakistan offers breathtaking natural beauty. The vibrant cities of Lahore and Karachi are known for their historical sites, bustling bazaars, and culinary delights.

With a heritage that reflects a mix of various civilizations, Pakistan is home to ancient ruins like Mohenjo-Daro and the stunning Badshahi Mosque. Adventure seekers can explore the northern regions for trekking and mountaineering, while cultural enthusiasts can immerse themselves in the traditions of Sindh and Punjab. Pakistan’s hospitality and warm-hearted people make it a memorable destination.

Transportation in Pakistan

Air Travel

Major international airports include Jinnah International Airport (KHI) in Karachi and Islamabad International Airport (ISB).

Public Transit

Buses and rickshaws are common modes of public transportation in urban areas.

Car Rental

Car rentals are available through various companies in major cities.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Careem and Uber are popular ride-sharing options in major cities.
