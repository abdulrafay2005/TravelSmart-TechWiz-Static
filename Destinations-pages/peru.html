OVERVIEW

Peru is a land of incredible diversity, offering rich cultural history, stunning landscapes, and world-renowned cuisine. From the majestic ruins of Machu Picchu to the vibrant streets of Lima, Peru is a destination that captivates travelers seeking adventure, history, and natural beauty. The country is famous for its ancient Incan heritage, diverse ecosystems, and vibrant traditions.

Whether you're trekking through the Andes, cruising along the Amazon River, or enjoying a delicious meal of ceviche, Peru offers a unique experience for every traveler. The Sacred Valley, Lake Titicaca, and the coastal desert with the mysterious Nazca Lines are just a few of the incredible places to explore.

Transportation in Peru

Air Travel

Main airports include Jorge Chávez International Airport (LIM) in Lima and Alejandro Velasco Astete Airport (CUZ) in Cusco.

Public Transit

Buses and combis are the main modes of public transportation, with extensive networks across cities like Lima and Cusco.

Car Rental

Rent cars from major companies like Hertz or Budget, especially for trips to rural areas or regions around Cusco.

Taxis and Ride-Sharing

Uber, Cabify, and local taxis are widely used, especially in Lima and other urban centers.

Accommodation Options

Luxury Hotels

Indulge in 5-star luxury at hotels in Lima, Cusco, and Sacred Valley, offering world-class amenities and breathtaking views.

Budget Hostels

Affordable and cozy hostels, perfect for budget-conscious travelers exploring Peru’s stunning landscapes.

Bed & Breakfast

Stay in traditional, family-run B&Bs offering personalized experiences, especially around Cusco and the Sacred Valley.

Vacation Rentals

Rent a home or apartment in cities like Lima or Cusco for a more local experience.

Location of Peru

Peru is located in western South America, bordered by Ecuador and Colombia to the north, Brazil and Bolivia to the east, and Chile to the south. It features diverse landscapes, from the Pacific Ocean coastline to the Amazon rainforest and Andean mountain ranges.
